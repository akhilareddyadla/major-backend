    Product Description
    Honey Comb Lock-In Technology

    This Whirlpool Direct Cool Single Door refrigerator has a large vegetable crisper backed by Honey Comb Lock-In technology to maintain optimum moisture inside the refrigerator. Thanks to the Honey Comb Moisture Lock-In technology, you can keep your fruits and vegetables fresh for a long time.

    Anti-bacterial Gasket

    This refrigerator features an anti-bacterial gasket which makes cleaning hassle-free. You can remove this airtight gasket while cleaning the door liner. This anti-bacterial gasket prevents the build-up of bacteria and fungi so that your food stays fresh and hygienic.

    Easy Defrosting

    Thanks to the manual defrosting mechanism this refrigerator features, you can easily choose to defrost as and when required. This helps to offer enhanced cooling and helps to keep your food fresh for long.

    Stabiliser-free Operations

    You can enjoy the stabiliser-free operations of this refrigerator as it can function even in high voltage fluctuations between 95 V to 300 V. You do not have to purchase a separate stabiliser with this refrigerator.

    Insulated Capillary Technology

    The Insulated Capillary technology ensures the cooling inside this refrigerator retains for up to 9 hours. The capillary which carries the refrigerant from the compressor to the freezer is surrounded by cold gas to offer enhanced efficiency and fast cooling.

    Large Bottle Storage

    This refrigerator has two door racks specially designed to store bottles so you can easily big and small bottles alike.

    Large Freezer

    With the huge freezer capacity, this refrigerator allows you to store food in large quantities so you never run out of space to store your frozen foods.

    Large Vegetable Tray

    Thanks to the large vegetable tray featured in this refrigerator, you can store all your groceries and even have space for more.
